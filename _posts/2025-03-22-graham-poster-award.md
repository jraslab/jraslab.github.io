Undergraduate research Graham Robertson recently attended the [58th Annual Northwest Developmental Biology Meeting](https://www.nwdbmeeting.org) in Friday Harbor, WA. At the conference, Graham presented his work on skin stem heterogeneity in zebrafish. For his poster presentation, Graham received a **2nd place undergraduate award**, sponsored by the [Society for Developmental Biology](https://www.sdbonline.org). Congratulations, Graham!
